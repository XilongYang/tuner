Azure Speech credentials

Your key is stored only in this browser (localStorage) and is never uploaded to any server. This tool has no backend — every request goes directly from your browser to Azure. That means your key is visible in the Network panel of the browser dev tools, which is expected (it is your own key, and you are responsible for its usage and billing).
